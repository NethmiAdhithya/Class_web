Grade 04 - SITHIJA

The Grade 04 Class serves as a critical stage in a student’s academic journey, focusing on building a deeper understanding of fundamental subjects and introducing intermediate concepts. The curriculum emphasizes analytical thinking, collaborative learning, and practical application of knowledge in subjects such as Mathematics, Science, and Languages. Students are encouraged to participate in discussions, projects, and activities that foster intellectual curiosity and confidence. The Grade 04 Class aims to prepare students for the challenges of advanced studies while promoting holistic growth in academic, social, and creative aspects.

Sinhala Language

Expands on language skills with a focus on advanced vocabulary, comprehension, and creative writing. Students will explore poetry and prose, enhancing their appreciation of Sinhala literature.

Mathematics

Introduces concepts such as fractions, decimals, and measurement. Problem-solving exercises and real-world applications help students understand the practical use of mathematics in daily life.

Science

Encourages scientific thinking through topics like plants, animals, weather, and the human body. Hands-on experiments and activities foster curiosity and a deeper understanding of natural phenomena.

English Language

Develops fluency in reading, writing, and speaking. Students learn grammar, storytelling, and basic essay writing, improving their ability to communicate effectively in English.

History and Geography

Introduces key historical events and geographical concepts such as maps, landscapes, and cultural diversity. This subject helps students understand the world around them and their place within it.
