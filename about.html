About Us


Bio:

An experienced and passionate educator with a deep commitment to fostering academic excellence and personal growth in students. Having dedicated nearly two decades to the field of education, the tutor has developed a profound understanding of primary-level teaching and administration.


Educational Background:

Primary education at Am/Paragahakale Vidyalaya.
Successfully completed Ordinary Level (O/L) and Advanced Level (A/L) studies at Am/Gamini National College.


Professional Qualifications and Experience:

Completed Primary Teacher Training, earning a comprehensive understanding of teaching methodologies and child development.
Appointed as a Primary School Teacher, demonstrating outstanding teaching capabilities and a strong dedication to students’ success.
Accumulated an impressive 18 years of service in the education sector, with extensive experience in both teaching and school leadership.
Currently serving as the Principal of Am/Koknahara Primary School, overseeing school operations and guiding staff and students toward excellence.


Philosophy:

Believes in nurturing young minds with compassion and innovative teaching practices, creating a foundation for lifelong learning and achievement.
